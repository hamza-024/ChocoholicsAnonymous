Design Paragraph:

Our ChocAn System design embodies high cohesion and low coupling, foundational principles for robust software design. The high cohesion is evident: classes like Verify Operator, provider, and manager are logically grouped by their shared verification function, while Member and Provider Management, despite sharing classes like Terminal and Database, maintain distinct data access routes. This promotes clarity and ease of maintenance. On the coupling side, modules like Verify provider, operator, and manager share functions but operate independently, preventing cascading errors from changes in one module. Similarly, terminals for Operator, Manager, and Provider, though sharing an interface, retain distinct functionalities. Report functions, though reliant on Member and Provider data, access unique data streams, preserving system flexibility. In essence, the synergy of high cohesion and low coupling in our design ensures a balance between functionality and modularity, making ChocAn resilient and adaptable.
